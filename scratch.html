Save the Bees!

3 Facts About Bees:

  1. Bees Pollinate
    • The majority of plants depend on bees for pollination
    • Most of the food we eat depends on pollinators like bees
  2. Queen Bees Are Important
    • Each hive has only one queen bee
    • The queen's only job is to lay eggs
  3. You Can Help the Bees
    • Planting bee friendly plants and flowers can help the bee population
    • You can spread awareness about the importance of bees to friends and family
